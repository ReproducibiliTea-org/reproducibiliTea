We are a group of student organizers from the University of California, Santa Barbara, and have been hosting ReproducibiliTea workshops at UCSB since 2023.

Over this period, we have organized events on topics such as publication bias and preregistration, as well as hands-on workshops focused on version control. We are committed to fostering interdisciplinary dialogue across fields including psychology, political science, and economics. In addition, we actively engage with leading open science researchers from other institutions, learning from their expertise and experiences.

Our overarching goal is to promote open science practices and advance reproducible research within our local academic community.
